2004 Race Summary

On 4.16.04 the second annual RDR was held on the CMU Track.  A large turnout was rewarded with gorgeous, sunny weather.  As the starting time approached, the 50 runners anxiously waited as Steven Rudich rolled the first of the ENORMOUS FUZZY DICE - a two.  The runners breathed a sigh of relief and anticipated a short race.  Many started out quite quickly - with the leader running the first lap in 67 seconds!  Very quickly, the second die was rolled, and with a gasp, it turned up a six... Quickly adjusting, many runners slowed their pace and settled in for the long haul - painfully giving up all hopes of a short race.  The field began to spread and by the time the bell lap rang, Steve Back had a 5 second lead on the field and cruised towards home uncontested.  However, the team and student/advisor competitions were yet to be decided.  Electical Engineering put in a dominant performance in dethroning the reigning theory champions.  And Adrian Perrig and Jonathon McCune won a very hotly contested student/advisor competition that featured 4 teams within 20 points of each other.
